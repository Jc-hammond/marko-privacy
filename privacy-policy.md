# Marko Privacy Policy

*Effective date: June 11, 2026*

Marko is a Markdown viewer and editor for macOS, made by Connor Hammond.

## The short version

Marko collects nothing. It has no network features, no analytics, no
accounts, and no third-party services. Your documents never leave your Mac.

## What Marko does with your data

- **Your documents** are read and written only on your Mac, only for files
  and folders you explicitly open, and only when you explicitly save or
  export. Marko runs in the macOS App Sandbox, which technically prevents
  it from accessing anything you haven't opened.
- **Remembered locations** (your last-opened folder and pinned files) are
  stored as macOS security-scoped bookmarks in Marko's local preferences on
  your Mac. They are never transmitted anywhere.
- **Rendering** happens entirely on-device using libraries bundled inside
  the app. Marko makes no network requests of any kind.

## What Marko collects

Nothing. No personal information, no usage analytics, no crash reports, no
identifiers, no telemetry.

## Third parties

Marko embeds no third-party SDKs and shares data with no one, because it
collects no data.

## Changes

If a future version of Marko ever changes any of the above, this policy
will be updated before that version ships, and the change will be noted in
the release notes.
